Research Interest

My research focuses on the security of Cyber-Physical Systems(CPS), with a strong emphasis on sensor security , including the detection and mitigation of sensor spoofing and integrity attacks on sensing and control components. During my undergraduate studies, I completed a thesis on blockchain technologies, which motivated my interest in distributed trust and system security.
